Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Folio (生灵集), a nature observation app that turns chance encounters with living things into lasting memories. Folio is operated by an individual developer ("we," "us," or "our").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Folio iOS application. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the app.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Email address — collected when you sign in with Apple or Google. In the case of Sign in with Apple, you may choose to hide your real email address; we will receive the relay address Apple provides.
  • Memory notes — optional text you write to accompany a species encounter (e.g., "Spotted on my morning walk in Irvine").

Information collected automatically

  • Photos / Camera — images you capture or select from your photo library for species identification. Your original photo is uploaded to our servers to process the identification request.
  • Location (GPS) — the approximate location at the time of a sighting, used to record where you encountered a species and to display your encounter map. Location is accessed only when you actively use the identification feature.
  • Timestamps — the date and time of each species encounter, extracted from your photo metadata or recorded at the moment of identification.
  • Device information — basic device identifiers and OS version, used for debugging and compatibility purposes.
We do not collect your first name, last name, phone number, mailing address, or social media profile information. We do not use Google Analytics or other third-party analytics trackers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Identify species in photos you submit and generate watercolor portraits of those species.
  • Build and display your personal Collection (My Folio), including encounter location, time, and species information.
  • Associate your data with your account so it persists across devices and sessions.
  • Enforce usage limits for free-tier users (e.g., monthly identification quotas).
  • Process in-app subscription purchases via Apple's in-app payment system.
  • Respond to support inquiries sent to folio.foraging@gmail.com.
  • Improve the app's species identification accuracy and overall user experience.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for remarketing or advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

Folio uses the following third-party services to operate:

Google Gemini API

Your photos are transmitted to Google's Gemini API for species identification and watercolor image generation. Google processes this data under its own privacy policies. We do not store your raw photos on our servers beyond the time required to complete the identification request. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Supabase

We use Supabase as our database provider to store your account information, encounter history, and generated portraits. Data is stored in secure cloud infrastructure. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.

Cloudflare R2

Generated watercolor portraits and related image assets are stored using Cloudflare R2 object storage. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Sign in with Apple / Google Sign-In

Authentication is handled by Apple or Google. We receive only the email address (or relay address) and a unique identifier from these providers. We do not receive your password.

Apple In-App Purchases

All subscription payments are processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment card information. Apple's payment handling is governed by Apple's own privacy policies.

Location Data

Folio requests access to your device's location to record where species encounters occur. This information is stored as part of your encounter records and displayed on your personal map within the app.

  • Location is accessed only when you actively use the identification feature, not continuously in the background.
  • You may deny location access; the app will still function, but encounter location will not be recorded.
  • We do not share your precise location data with third parties for advertising or analytics purposes.

Camera & Photo Access

Folio requests access to your camera and photo library to allow you to photograph and identify species. Photos you submit for identification are transmitted to the Gemini API for processing. We do not store your original photos on our servers after identification is complete. The watercolor portrait generated from your photo is stored separately and does not contain the original image.

Data Retention

We retain your account data and encounter history for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

To request deletion of your data, contact us at folio.foraging@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Folio is intended for users who are 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. By using the app, you represent that you are at least 13 years old.

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take prompt steps to delete such information from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at folio.foraging@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights Under CCPA / CPRA (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), effective January 1, 2023:

  • Right to Know — You have the right to request information about the personal data we have collected about you, the sources of that data, the purposes for which it is used, and the categories of third parties with whom it is shared.
  • Right to Delete — You have the right to request deletion of personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Correct — You have the right to request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing — We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at folio.foraging@gmail.com. We will respond to verifiable requests within 45 days.

Folio does not have actual knowledge that it sells or shares personal information of California consumers under 16 years of age.

Your Rights Under GDPR (EU / EEA Residents)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access — You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification — You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure — You may request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to Restriction of Processing — You may request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability — You may request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object — You may object to our processing of your personal data.

Our legal basis for processing your data is the performance of our contract with you (i.e., providing the Folio service). To exercise any of these rights, contact us at folio.foraging@gmail.com.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

CalOPPA (California Online Privacy Protection Act)

In accordance with CalOPPA, effective January 1, 2014, we agree to the following:

  • Users can visit Folio anonymously (prior to sign-in).
  • This Privacy Policy link is accessible from the app's settings and any relevant store listing page.
  • We will notify users of any material Privacy Policy changes by updating the effective date at the top of this document.
  • Users can update their personal information by contacting us at folio.foraging@gmail.com or by deleting their account within the app.

We honor "Do Not Track" signals and do not track users across third-party websites.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and secure cloud infrastructure provided by Supabase and Cloudflare. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
